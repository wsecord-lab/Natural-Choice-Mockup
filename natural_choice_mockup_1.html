Our story

Rooted in Oakland
Since 1997

The Natural Choice has been a fixture at 109 Meyran Ave for nearly three decades. What started as a small neighborhood shop has grown into one of Oakland's most trusted spots for precision fades, clean lines, and natural hair care.

We've had the privilege of shaping up generations of students, locals, and everyone in between. Walk-ins welcome — appointments always preferred.
