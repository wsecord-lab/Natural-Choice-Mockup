Our story

Rooted in Oakland
Since 1997

The Natural Choice has been a fixture at 109 Meyran Ave for nearly three decades. What started as a small neighborhood shop has grown into one of Oakland's most trusted spots for precision fades, clean lines, and natural hair care.

We've had the privilege of shaping up generations of students, locals, and everyone in between. Walk-ins welcome — appointments always preferred.

Nate
Master Barber · Est. 1997

Nate has been perfecting his craft since before most of his clients were born. With over 36 years behind the chair, he built The Natural Choice from the ground up and turned it into one of Oakland's most beloved neighborhood institutions. His philosophy is simple: every person who sits in the chair deserves to leave feeling their best.
